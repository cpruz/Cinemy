About Cinemy:

Cinemy allows users to learn about some of the most popular and critically acclaimed movies in the world. Users can share their opinion on hundreds of thousands of movies and view other users' thoughts as well. Cinemy provides the ability to find the Most Popular, Now Playing, and Top Rated movies while also having the option to search for any movie as well!
